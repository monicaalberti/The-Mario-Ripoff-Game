How to play

Purpose of the game

You have to keep moving to the right until you reach the star. Once you reach the star you have won the game.

During the game you also have to collect as many coins as you can, as the number of coins you collect will determine your final score.

Arrows

  • Arrow Right →
    To move the player to the right.
  • Arrow Left ←
    To move the player to the left.
  • Arrow Up ↑
    To jump.

Enemies

Grumpy Dragon

Make sure to jump over it when it flies your way!

Crocodile

Careful not to fall in the water!

Space Aliens

They fly in formation. Be careful!

Asteroids

You will encounter these a couple of times throughout the game so watch out for them!
